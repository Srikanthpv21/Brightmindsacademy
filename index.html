Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers.

We believe in personalized attention. To ensure every student gets the focus they deserve, we keep our batch sizes small, typically with a maximum of 8-10 students per class.

We strongly encourage attending all live classes for the best interactive experience. If a student misses a session, we provide the class notes and they can ask any questions during the next session to catch up. Please note that we do not provide recordings of the classes.

All you need is a stable internet connection, a computer or laptop with a webcam and microphone, and a modern web browser like Google Chrome. For programming courses, we will guide you through the simple process of installing any necessary free software.
