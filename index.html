Frequently Asked Questions

We believe in personalized attention. To ensure every student gets the focus they deserve, we keep our batch sizes small, typically with a maximum of 8-10 students per class.

We provide class notes for any missed session, and students can clarify doubts in the next class. We strongly encourage live attendance for the best interactive experience. We do not provide class recordings to protect student privacy.

All you need is a stable internet connection, a computer or laptop with a webcam and microphone. For programming courses, we will guide you through the simple process of installing any necessary free software.
